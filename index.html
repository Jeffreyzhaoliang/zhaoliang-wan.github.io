I'm a first-year Computer Science PhD student at Sun Yat-sen University. I have always been fascinated by the interaction between robots and the real-world. Branching out from this theme, I have explored underwater visual perception and autonomous grasping system for open compound ocean environments.

I'm fortunate to be supervised by Professor Hui Cheng, and co-advised by Associate Professor Xu Yang at CASIA. My research will focus on robotic manipulation and perception.

Exploring new things has been the greatest source of pleasure, and I hope this will never stop. Recently, I have been practising kayak and canoe in my free time.
