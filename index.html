I am a Computer Science PhD student at Sun Yat-sen University in the Robotics Lab . I am also a research student at Tencent Robotics X.

I have always been fascinated by the interaction between universal robots and the real-world. Before coming to SYSU, I explored visual perception and grasping of underwater robots. My research will focus on robotic manipulation and interactive perception.

I'm fortunate to be supervised by Professor Hui Cheng, and co-advised by Associate Professor Xu Yang.

Fitness helps me keep vigorous, and inspires me to push my limits. Recently, I have been practising kayaking and swimming in my free time.
